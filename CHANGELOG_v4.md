# Changelog - Sofia Core v4.0.0

## [4.0.0] - 2026-02-08

### 🌐 Paradigm Shift: Distributed Sovereign Intelligence

#### Distributed Architecture
- ✨ **Mesh Networking**: P2P node communication with auto-discovery
- ✨ **Distributed Coordination**: Multi-node task distribution with consensus
- ✨ **Blockchain Consensus**: Tamper-proof distributed state management
- ✨ **Service Discovery**: Dynamic service registration and discovery
- ✨ **Zero-Downtime Failover**: Automatic node failover and recovery

#### Quantum-Ready Security
- ✨ **Post-Quantum Encryption**: CRYSTALS-Kyber implementation
- ✨ **Quantum-Safe Signatures**: CRYSTALS-Dilithium support
- ✨ **Zero-Knowledge Proofs**: ZK-SNARKs for privacy-preserving verification
- ✨ **Future-Proof Cryptography**: NTRU, FALCON algorithms

#### Multi-Modal AI
- ✨ **Vision + Voice + Text**: Unified multimodal processing
- ✨ **Cross-Modal Reasoning**: Semantic understanding across modalities
- ✨ **Content-Only Analysis**: Non-biometric vision and audio processing

#### New Domain Forks
- ✨ **Finance Fork** (Port 8005): Compliance, risk analysis, fraud detection
  - Regulatory frameworks: SEC, FINRA, Basel III, MiFID II
  - AML screening, KYC verification, sanctions checking
  - Credit, market, operational, liquidity risk analysis
  - Transaction monitoring and anomaly detection
  - **NO financial advice | NO investment recommendations**

- ✨ **Government Fork** (Port 8006): Public service, policy analysis
  - Citizen services and case management
  - Policy research and impact assessment
  - Transparency tools and open data support
  - Civic engagement and digital services
  - **NO political advocacy | Nonpartisan analysis ONLY**

- ✨ **Medical Research Fork** (Port 8007): Non-clinical research only
  - Literature search (PubMed integration)
  - Statistical analysis and data visualization
  - Epidemiological modeling
  - Research collaboration and protocol development
  - **NO clinical diagnosis | NO medical advice | Research ONLY**
