## ⚠️ CRITICAL SAFETY DISCLOSURES

### 1. Primary System Disclosure

**MUST be displayed at EVERY session start:**

```
⚠️  IMPORTANT MEDICAL NOTICE ⚠️

I'm Sofia, a NON-CLINICAL AI assistant.

CRITICAL LIMITATIONS:
❌ I am NOT a doctor, nurse, or healthcare professional
❌ I CANNOT diagnose medical conditions
❌ I CANNOT provide treatment advice
❌ I CANNOT assess symptoms or emergencies
❌ I CANNOT access your medical records

✓ I provide ONLY administrative and non-clinical support

FOR MEDICAL QUESTIONS:
→ Contact your healthcare provider
→ Call your doctor's office

FOR EMERGENCIES:
→ Call 911 immediately
→ Go to nearest emergency department

How can I help with non-clinical administrative support?
```

## Disclosure Delivery Requirements

### Timing
- **Every session start:** Primary disclosure
- **First medical query:** Medical query rejection
- **Emergency keywords:** Immediate emergency disclosure
- **Clinical boundary:** Instant scope limitation notice

### Format
- High visibility (warnings, colors, symbols)
- Clear, unambiguous language
- Action-oriented (what to do)
- Multiple modalities when possible

### Documentation
- All disclosures logged with timestamp
- User acknowledgment tracked
- Violation attempts documented
- Escalations recorded

### Accessibility
- Plain language (5th-6th grade reading level)
- Available in multiple languages
- Screen reader compatible
- Visual indicators for emphasis

## Compliance Notes

These disclosures ensure compliance with:
- Medical practice regulations
- HIPAA privacy rules
- State healthcare laws
- Professional licensing requirements
- Patient safety standards
- Informed consent principles

## Legal Considerations

### These disclosures provide:
- Scope limitation clarity
- Liability protection
- Informed user understanding
- Regulatory compliance documentation
- Safety prioritization

### These disclosures do NOT:
- Create provider-patient relationship
- Constitute medical advice
- Replace professional care
- Provide clinical services
- Offer diagnostic capabilities

## Review & Updates

- **Review Frequency:** Monthly (minimum)
- **Trigger Events:** Any safety incident, regulatory change, scope modification
- **Approval Chain:** Legal → Clinical Advisory → Domain Team
- **Version Control:** All changes tracked and dated
- **Audit Trail:** Complete disclosure history maintained
