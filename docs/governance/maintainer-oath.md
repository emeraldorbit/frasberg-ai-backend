# Sofia Core SDK — Maintainer Oath

As a maintainer of the Sofia Core SDK, I affirm the following:

## 1. I am a steward of the architecture
I will protect the structure, identity, and invariants of the SDK.  
I will ensure every change aligns with the system's core principles.

## 2. I uphold minimalism with discipline
I will reject unnecessary features.  
I will prevent complexity from creeping into the codebase.  
I will preserve the SDK's clarity and simplicity.

## 3. I enforce predictability
I will not allow hidden behavior.  
I will not allow silent fallbacks.  
I will not allow nondeterminism.

**Predictability is trust.**

## 4. I guard provider neutrality
I will not privilege any provider.  
I will not allow provider-specific logic to leak.  
I will maintain strict abstraction boundaries.

**Neutrality is integrity.**

## 5. I protect the user above all
I will ensure no secrets leak.  
I will enforce strict validation.  
I will sanitize all outputs.  
I will fail fast and fail clean.

**Security is responsibility.**

## 6. I maintain the highest standards of quality
I will enforce testing.  
I will enforce documentation.  
I will enforce code review rigor.  
I will enforce the quality gates.

**Quality is non-negotiable.**

## 7. I uphold the governance charter
I will follow the RFC process.  
I will respect the branching model.  
I will maintain transparent decision-making.  
I will resolve disputes with fairness and clarity.

**Governance is stability.**

## 8. I evolve the SDK intentionally
I will plan releases with care.  
I will avoid architectural drift.  
I will ensure every change strengthens the system.

**Evolution must be deliberate.**

## 9. I communicate with clarity and respect
I will support contributors.  
I will provide constructive feedback.  
I will maintain a welcoming environment.

**Stewardship is service.**

## 10. I preserve the identity of the SDK
I will not compromise minimalism for convenience.  
I will not compromise determinism for speed.  
I will not compromise neutrality for expedience.  
I will not compromise clarity for cleverness.

**Identity is the foundation.**

---

This oath represents the final human-governance artifact in the Sofia Core SDK stewardship framework.
